Fingerprint Scanner Virdi BioSeal-P

The Virdi Bioseal-P Fingerprint Sensor is a versatile biometric device designed for secure and fast fingerprint authentication. It supports multi-purpose fingerprint registration and can store up to 10 access permissions, such as passwords, for each registered fingerprint. With a high-speed processor (192 MHz) and 500 DPI fingerprint quality, it ensures rapid and accurate user verification. Ideal for financial and industrial systems, the Bioseal-P enhances security while providing seamless website access integration. Powered via USB (5V), it operates efficiently in diverse conditions (0–50°C, up to 80% humidity). Compact (38×70×31 mm) and lightweight (99 g), it includes Wiegand and RS485 connectivity.

Fingerprint Scanner Virdi FOH02

The Virdi FOH02 USB Fingerprint Reader Series is a versatile line of biometric authentication devices designed for secure and efficient fingerprint recognition. These devices are equipped with advanced fingerprint scanning algorithms for fast and precise performance, supporting seamless integration with Microsoft Windows operating systems via Plug-and-Play functionality. Each model in the series is fully compatible with UNIS Access Control Management software, enhancing its versatility for various professional environments. With a compact design (74×92×84 mm) and USB-powered operation, the FOH02 series is both portable and reliable.

  1. The Virdi FOH02 USB Fingerprint model is a standalone fingerprint scanner without a card reader, offering 500 DPI resolution for accurate biometric data capture.
  2. The Virdi FOH02 RF USB Fingerprint includes a built-in 125KHz RFID card reader, making it ideal for environments that require both biometric and RFID-based access control.
  3. The Virdi FOH02 SC USB Fingerprint takes security further with a 13.56 MHz Mifare and DESFire contactless smart card reader, providing advanced compatibility for secure identification in sophisticated systems.

These three models ensure flexibility and scalability for a wide range of security and access control applications, meeting the diverse needs of businesses and institutions.
