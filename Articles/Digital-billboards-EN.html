Humans use LED displays of any size to find a better market for selling and introducing their products. As people spend most of their time in cars to go to work or travel, installing an LED display screen is very important in the right place to see people. Today, cities of our country as the many countries of the world, colorful LEDs in shapes and various types assign highways in urban areas. The installation of the LED displays on the road border has been considered a common problem in most cities.

The primary use of LED display is in the shopping center because advertisers like their products are in the public view and introduced. One of the most important reasons why LED-backlit display is used in advertising is that they can change messages instantly with our preferences. An investigation conducted in 182 COMPUSA stores in 2004-2005 showed that the average sales of advertised goods increased by 29%. Experts believe that an average of 10% to 20% more sales occurred in places that used LED displays.

Network Equipment of LED Displays

It is necessary to launch an LED display to electronic display multimedia systems compatible network and content management software that can update content remotely. Digital content in these networks driven from a central server from the internet. Now LCD and plasma screens are used for inner advertising and LED display use in the city.

Advantages of LED Displays

  • Messages can be changed quickly, and people can be exposed to the latest news and events.
  • Without any physical and human resources, messages can be updated remotely in a fraction of a second.
  • The main function of advertising LED displays are beyond advertising because they can be used as an educational tool in the office. So employees and customers can be aware of the latest news and events. For example, LEDs are displayed hours and calendar in banks or introduced new items in stores or a hospital's turning system. Depending on the type of people, a message can be displayed to their age or their work.
  • Since the most popular use of LEDs is in shopping malls, updating information and prices is very important for business owners to establish a good relationship with customers.
