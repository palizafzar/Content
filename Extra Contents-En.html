TFace 901 Face and Fingerprint Time Attendance System

TFace 901 is a multi-biometric time & attendance and access control system. It is equipped with a 2.8" color display, 16 functional keys, a contactless card reader, especially 125 KHz and Mifare 13.56 MHz, ARM Quad-core CPU, and fingerprint recognition. This amazing time and attendance terminal supports 5,000 users, 100,000 logs, 1,000 faces, and 5,000 fingerprints as well as cards. It is able to communicate with other systems by LAN and USB ports. With a built-in two infrared, it recognizes facials as speedy.

AC6000 Fingerprint Time Attendance and Access Control System

AC6000 is one of Virdi's showcase biometric access control that contains the latest technology. It is implemented with a large 4.8-inch color touch screen display, a built-in digital camera, and a speedy processor. AC6000 also comes with Voice over IP (VoIP) technology that notifies your secretary when a VIP guest is waiting at the door. This fingerprint time and attendance system is equipped with 30,000 fingerprints and 100,000 cards recognition. It is outfitted with a large 4.8″ Color TFT touch screen. The sparkling display allows device administrators easy access to all the settings without using keypads. The configuration is intuitive with the touch screen, and the device can be set up much quicker.

With a built-in USB port, this access control terminal allows device administrators to backup and transfer settings only with standard USB thumb drives. Settings can be backed up, especially configurations, firmware, and image & transaction logs. Additionally, biometric AC6000 will recognize your VIP face shot and notify your secretary through connected VoIP devices. Your VIP can be granted access through the door without any wait time or inconvenience.

AC7000 Multi-Biometric Time Attendance and Access Control System

AC7000 is the latest access controller with fake finger detection and a tilt camera, which automatically recognizes a face within 3m. Its 5-inch LCD touch screen and friendly Android smartphone application significantly improve device management and user convenience. Storage capacity is expandable by an external SD-card that supports 250,000 users.
